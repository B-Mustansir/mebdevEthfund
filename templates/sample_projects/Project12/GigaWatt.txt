GigaWatt - a Power Play for the Future

Can you lead your European Region to a Clean Energy Future? A strategic 1-6 player game of Technology, Investing, Trading and Auctions.
GigaWatt is an energy-themed strategic board game in which you lead your European region to a clean energy future. Build power plants and trade electricity to become the first to fulfil the energy transition!
During your journey you face realistic challenges: explosive growth of electricity demand, protests in your region, negotiations with other regions and decisions between technologies...
