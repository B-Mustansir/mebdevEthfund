Emora is a high fantasy adventure comic book series set in the Dark Ages of a Bronze Era. It follows a young girl named Emora who is born with mysterious powers and destined by myth to be taken and never seen again. Palithea, her mother, does everything she can to protect her daughter from what's both outside and inside the walls of their village.
