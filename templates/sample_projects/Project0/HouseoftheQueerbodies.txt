House of the Queerbodies: Part 1

An all ages comic! Ellen travels to a world where fairy tales and nursery rhymes are real to find the story her grandmother forgot.
House of the Queerbodies is a graphic novel illustrated by Robin Richardson, adapted by Taylor Curreysmith from the book As the Goose Flies by Katharine Pyle, and edited by Jasmine Walls.
Ellen is a girl caught in a world where fairy tales are real on a quest to find the story her grandmother has forgotten.
