OUR JOURNEY

Hi, we are a group of 5 first year students at VIT Pune. We created this project as a part of our Capstone Project. This is a crowdfunding website based on Blockchain Technology. We hope you like it.

OUR MISSION

Redefining the Way we Create and Fund Projects.

Crowdfunding can be made a sole revenue model for a journalist or a publication, and it can replace the legacy business models for journalism that enforces prejudiced journalism leading to favoritism. At times when independent journalists are struggling to fund serious journalism, the advent of crowdfunding is a welcome development.

The motivation of this project was to create a platform where these individuals can comfortably raise enough capital for unbiased and impartial journalism that benefits the society. Blockchain technology helps in creating a decentralized network of users where the transactions are recorded in an open distributed ledger. These features of blockchain enable a transparent and cost-effective platform for different applications.
